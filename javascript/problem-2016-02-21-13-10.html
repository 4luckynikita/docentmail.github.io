Question: What is percentage of support ECMAScript 6.0 in modern browsers?

Tip: Support is pretty poor.


Answer: Percentage varies from version to version of the browser.

  • Desktop:
    • Chrome 63-91%
    • Edge 62-82%
  • Mobile:
    • Android 12-28%
    • iOS 11-54%
